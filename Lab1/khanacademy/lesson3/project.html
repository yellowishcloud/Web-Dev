Recipe #1 Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4-6
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400 grams
Ground beef 500 grams
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Onion 1 medium, chopped
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Italian seasoning 1 tablespoon
Olive oil 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

Step 2: In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onions and minced garlic until golden.

Step 3: Add ground beef to the pan and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.

Step 4: Pour in tomato sauce, add Italian seasoning, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.

Step 5: Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Recipe #2 Chicken Stir Fry

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 500 grams, sliced
Broccoli 2 cups, florets
Carrots 2, julienned
Soy sauce 3 tablespoons
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Ginger 1 tablespoon, grated
Sesame oil 1 tablespoon

Step 1: In a wok or large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Add sliced chicken and cook until browned.

Step 3: Add minced garlic and grated ginger, sauté for 1-2 minutes.

Step 4: Add julienned carrots and broccoli florets. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp.

Step 5: Pour soy sauce over the stir fry, toss to combine, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

Recipe #3 Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 ball
Tomatoes 2, sliced
Fresh mozzarella 200 grams, sliced
Fresh basil 1/2 cup, leaves
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper To taste

Step 1: Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475°F or 245°C).

Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

Step 3: Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet.

Step 4: Arrange tomato slices and mozzarella on the dough. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.

Step 5: Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Top with fresh basil before serving.
